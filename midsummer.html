About the Play

The Story

Four young lovers flee into an enchanted forest, where mischievous fairies and magical spells turn one ordinary night into something extraordinary.

Shakespeare's beloved comedy weaves together three worlds — the court of Athens, the fairy realm, and a troupe of amateur actors — in a delightful tangle of love, confusion, and enchantment.

"The course of true love never did run smooth."
"Lord, what fools these mortals be!"
"I know a bank where the wild thyme blows..."
